Travel

Flights

For those flying, you have the choice of flying into Tbilisi or Kutaisi international airports. Though Kutaisi will require a 3-4 hour drive to Tbilisi so be prepared for a road trip. Flying with a layover at Istanbul cannot be avoided, especially when travelling from the UK, so with that in mind Turkish Airways and Pegasus are our airlines of choice.

Ceremony

The ceremony will be held one city over from Tbilisi, in the old capital; Mtskheta. We recommend taxiing, using either the Yandex taxi app or Bolt.

Reception

Krtsanisi residence is located within Tbilisi and again, we would recommend taxiing if you are not attending the religious ceremony. There will be buses available for those joining us at the religious ceremony to ferry you to the reception and its location allows easy availability of taxis at the end of the night.
