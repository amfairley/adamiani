Enjoy your stay in Georgia in style and experience world-class hospitality at this industrial-chic establishment located in the heart of Tbilisi, close to the shopping district. For more reasonable budgets, Tblisi Inn is the place to stay. Located within the quaint and recently renovated Turkish area of Tbilisi, there is no place better for a traditional coffee and cake for your early morning energy boost.


$ Cabinet Hotel $

For those on a budget, you cannot go wrong with the cosy Cabinet Hotel located just a short distance from the hustle and bustle of the city centre. We hope the comfortable rooms and great service isn't enough to stop you getting out of bed in the morning to explore the wonderful city.
